Benicio Isandro is a writer and poet with a penchant for all things macabre, paranormal, esoteric, and weird. Beni haunts medical labs by day and writes speculative flash fiction, haiku, senryu, and web code by night. Some of his work can be found in Failed Haiku, The Nottingham Horror Collective, and the upcoming inaugural issue of The Starlight SciFaiku Review.
